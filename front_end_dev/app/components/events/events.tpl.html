"52Hz -I love you" Movie Night at Culture Center
OCT10Mon
Tag 1 Tag 2

有可能在201”6”的芝加哥看到201”7”才上映的台灣電影？
這是有可能的！！

因為魏德聖導演與女主角要帶著《52Hz, I love you》來芝加哥了！
繼＜海角七號＞、＜賽德克巴萊＞、＜KANO＞後，魏導將帶著2017農曆年才要上映的電影《52Hz, I love you》來芝加哥放映。

芝加哥身為這次北美巡迴的一個重點站，不只魏導會親自陪我們看電影聊電影，劇中的女主角(保密中)也會一起前來芝加哥與大家一起看電影喔！

先來看看魏導有些話想跟北美的大家說～
(特別為了我們北美鄉親錄製的，附有英文字幕！)
YouTube連結→ https://youtu.be/R0KhGKTd_p8

TJCCC & TACCGC Annual Summer BBQ Surf and Turf !!!
TJCCC cordially invites all young professionals in Chicago to attend the 2016 Annual Summer BBQ event hosted by TACCGC (TJCCC's senior chapter). We will have abundance of delicious food and drinks, including suckling pig, lamb, seafood, Taiwanese cuisines, snacks, soda, wine, beer and many more!!.

This is a great opportunity for you to come hang out with successful senior entrepreneurs and network in a lay back setting . Come join us and and make new friends!!
