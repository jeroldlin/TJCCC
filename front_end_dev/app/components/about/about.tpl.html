About Us

Taiwanese Junior Chamber of Commerce Chicago

TJCCC is a non-profit organization under IRC Section 501(c)(3) and affiliated with TJCCNA (Taiwanese Junior Chamber of Commerce North America).

The mission of the organization is: 1) to establish a professional and social network in the City of Chicago, 2) to promote entrepreneurship and career goals, 3) to promote mutual contacts, friendship, and exchange of information and ideas among TJCCC members, 4) to assist each other in the development of their enterprises.
